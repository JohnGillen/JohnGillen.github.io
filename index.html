Pleroma

I run a Pleroma instance at networked.space called Networked Space.

  • 420 character limit
  • Free speech zone
  • Keyword filtering
  • Custom emoji (just ask)
  • Updated at least every month to the latest devel release

Icecast2

I also run an Icecast2 server and have a little Neocities site set up for it. If you want to be a DJ, just ask. We mostly play music but you can play whatever you want.

  • Strong, low-latency connection
  • Works with most DJ clients
  • Simple to setup
  • Play your music to potentially hundreds of people

Blog

A simple pelican blog where I write about whatever. Run on a Raspberry Pi and found at blog.networked.space
